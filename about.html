about

Hello, I am Giulia, a creative Italian living in London.
For the past 8 years I have been developing my creativity within the fashion industry, graduating in BA(hons) Fashion Design at Istituto Marangoni while working in retail and ultimately in e-commerce, where I have been getting involved in web development projects and found a new challenging way of expressing my creativity. That's where my journey into coding began.
I enjoy creating and building ideas to make people's life easier, without compromising a great aesthetic.
I am currently still working on e-commerce as well as different web development freelance projects.
