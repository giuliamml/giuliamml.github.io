Giulia Mummolo

I'm Giulia, a professional digital designer with a background in fashion design.
I have a passion for Front-End web development and interactive user interfaces.

In the past year, I’ve been working with as a technical consultant and full-stack developer for The Clothing Loop, a project in which I implement and manage a web app in React that will help a group of volunteers in a secondhand-clothes exchange program around the world. I’ve studied BA(hons) fashion design at Istituto Marangoni in London while working part-time in retail, for stores including Harrods where I was brand manager.
Also, I briefly worked as an e-commerce executive, an experience which fueled my interest for Front-End development.
